Tic-Tac-Toe

Rules

  1. Click on any empty tile to place a X.
  2. Win by getting three X's in a row either vertically, horizontally, or diagonally.
  3. Winning will add one point to your scoreboard, the same goes if the computer wins.
  4. A draw results in no points being added to either team.
  5. If you would like to reset the scoreboard, refresh the page or click on the refresh button in bottom right corner.
